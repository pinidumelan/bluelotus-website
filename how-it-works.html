How it works

Set it up once, then let it run

Hosting is enough work already. Once you are set up with us, your turnovers happen quietly in the background and your only job is to enjoy the reviews.

1

Share your dates

Link your Airbnb or Booking.com calendar so we can see your bookings, or simply send us checkout and check-in dates as they come in.

2

We turn it around

In the window between guests, our team cleans the whole property top to bottom, changes the linen and restocks the essentials.

3

We check and report

Before we leave, we give the place a final once-over and let you know about anything that needs attention, with photos on request.

4

Guests arrive to five stars

Your property is spotless, staged and ready to welcome the next guest, exactly as it should be, every time.

Built around your calendar

We work to your bookings, not the other way round

Every property runs to its own rhythm of arrivals and departures. We fit around yours, so a late checkout or a last-minute booking does not throw everything off.

  • Calendar linked or dates sent, whichever is easier for you.
  • Same-day windows handled, so back-to-back bookings are no problem.
  • You do not need to be there, we manage access ourselves.
  • Changes are easy, just let us know and we adjust.
