About Blue Lotus

Hosting made easier, one spotless changeover at a time

We started Blue Lotus because we saw how much of a host's time and stress goes into turnarounds. We wanted to take that off their hands and do it properly.

Our story

From helping one host to looking after dozens

It began with a single Airbnb host who was exhausted from racing between guests, washing linen late at night and hoping everything looked right before check-in. We stepped in, took over the changeovers, and their reviews started climbing.

Word got around. Before long we were helping more hosts, then property managers with whole portfolios. The job has grown, but the promise has not changed. We treat every property as if guests were arriving in an hour, because quite often they are.

No call centres and no faceless subcontractors. Just a dependable team and someone who knows your property when you need to talk.
