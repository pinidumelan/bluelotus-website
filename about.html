About Blue Lotus Facilities

Two decades of hospitality, poured into every clean

We did not arrive at short-let cleaning by accident. We grew up in hotels, learned housekeeping the hard way, and brought that standard with us.

Our story

From hotel corridors to your front door

Our story starts in 2002, on the housekeeping floors of busy hotels. Hundreds of rooms a day, inspections every shift, and guests who notice everything. That is where we learned what clean really means.

Over the years that followed we rose through the trade, leading housekeeping teams, training new staff and building the checklists and supervision that still power everything we do.

When short lets took off, hosts started asking for the same thing hotels enjoy. Blue Lotus Facilities is our answer. Hotel discipline, applied to Airbnbs, holiday lets and serviced apartments, delivered by staff trained to a high standard before they ever touch a property.

How we train

The five-way cleaning standard

Every member of staff is trained on the same five-step method before they clean a single property. It is why the standard never depends on who turns up.

1

Inspect

Walk the property first. Photograph damage, note anything missing, collect lost property.

2

Clean

Top to bottom, room by room, against a written checklist. Nothing done from memory.

3

Sanitise

High-touch points, bathrooms and kitchens disinfected properly, not just wiped.

4

Stage

Beds hotel-crisp, essentials restocked, welcome pack displayed, cushions just so.

5

Sign off

A final inspection against the checklist, signed by a supervisor before we leave.
